A b o u t

Current technological development has brought changes and development in the transportation. Online transportation service has become a means of transportation development which is able to attract people's interest. The online transportation service has created a convenient way for people to transport luggage. This project aims to create a platform for customers and drivers to realize their needs with a help of a mobile application. In this website, we have safe and secure registration by using government documentation, afterwards a driver and customer can access website as per their need. We are going to implement this website only for Kopargaon city. Both driver and customer are benefited in different way as our website provides the service in bidirectional way from source to the destination and on returning way driver will carry luggage of another customer so the driver is in profit and cost is reduced for customer. Our purposed website would be constantly helping people to transport their goods and luggage. This transportation will have some specific cost as per the km travelled by vehicle and some extra waiting charges.
